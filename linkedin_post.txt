🚀 Exciting news! I've just completed a Data Analysis Internship project on Restaurant Data Insights! 🍴📊

In this project for Cognifyz Technologies, I dived deep into a restaurant dataset to extract meaningful insights using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn. Here are some of the key takeaways:

🔹 Top Cuisines: North Indian, Chinese, and Fast Food dominate the market, together appearing in nearly 70% of the restaurants.
🔹 City Analysis: New Delhi has the highest restaurant count, but smaller cities like Inner City show higher average customer satisfaction.
🔹 Price vs Services: Premium restaurants (Price Range 4) prioritize table booking (fine-dining experience), while mid-range restaurants (Price Range 2) excel in online delivery.
🔹 Impact of Services: Restaurants offering online delivery tend to have significantly higher average ratings (3.25) compared to those without (2.47).
